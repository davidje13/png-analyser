pako_inflate.mjs is from https://github.com/nodeca/pako/blob/master/dist/pako_inflate.min.js
which is a port of zlib 1.2.8 by Vitaly Puzrin and Andrei Tuputcyn. It has had minor
modifications to work with ES6 module syntax.
